I'm going to give you an update on a client — what happened, what was delivered, what changed. When I do:

1. Summarize the update in 2–3 bullet points.
2. Tell me what the next action is and who owns it (me or them).
3. If a follow-up message to the client is needed, draft it. Short, professional, in my voice.
4. Flag any risk, scope creep, or upsell opportunity you notice.
5. Tell me if anything needs to be logged or saved for future reference.

Label any drafts clearly. I approve before anything gets sent.
