## FULL DESCRIPTION

You built your Business Brain. Now put it to work.

The Business Brain Workshop teaches Claude who you are — your business, your voice, your offers. The Command Pack gives you the commands to actually use it every day.

Without commands, you're still typing prompts from scratch every session. With them, you type one word and Claude handles the rest.

**This is how I run my own consulting business with Claude Code.** These are my real commands, adapted so they work for any service-based business — coaches, consultants, freelancers, anyone who has clients and needs to keep up with them.

---

**What's included:**

**`/daily-priorities`**
Run this every morning. Claude reads your client folder and Brain files and returns a structured action board — what to do today, in what order, with the single most important money move at the top.

**`/capture-message`**
Paste any incoming message — email, DM, inquiry, client reply. Claude classifies it, flags anything that needs your attention, and drafts a reply in your voice. You review and send.

**`/client-update`**
When a client interaction happens, paste what they said. Claude logs the update, flags scope changes or risks, and drafts a reply if one is needed.

**`/onboard-client`**
When someone signs or places an order, paste the details. Claude creates a structured client folder with everything it knows — and flags what's still missing.

**`/save-session`**
Run at the end of any work session. Claude reviews what happened, updates your memory files, and saves context so next session starts with full awareness — no re-explaining, no starting from scratch.

**`/route-offer`**
When a lead describes their situation, paste it here. Claude reads your services and pricing, matches them to the right offer, and drafts a reply recommending it.
