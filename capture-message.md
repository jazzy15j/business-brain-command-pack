I'm going to paste a message from a client, lead, or platform. When I do:

1. Tell me who this is and what they want — one sentence.
2. Classify it: New lead / Existing client / Follow-up / Support request / Complaint / Other.
3. Tell me the right move: reply now / wait / escalate / route to a product.
4. If a reply is needed, draft one in my voice. Match the tone of my business (from my brain file). Keep it short, clear, and professional. No filler.
5. Flag anything I should NOT say or promise based on what I know about my business.

Label the draft clearly. I approve before anything gets sent.
