I have a lead or an existing client and I need to figure out what to offer them. I'm going to describe their situation. When I do:

1. Tell me which of my services is the best fit and why — be specific.
2. Tell me the price to quote based on my pricing (confirm Fiverr vs. direct context if relevant).
3. Draft a short pitch or reply that presents the offer clearly — no overselling, no fluff.
4. Tell me what I should NOT offer or promise in this situation.
5. Flag any red flags or questions I should answer before sending anything.

Label any drafts clearly. I approve before anything gets sent.
