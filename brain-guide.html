The Solution

Three files. Everything Claude needs to know about you.

The Business Brain Workshop walks you through building three files that live in your project folder. Once they're built, Claude reads them at the start of every session — no re-explaining, no setup, no guessing.

📋
CLAUDE.md
Your Rules & Voice

Claude's instruction manual for working with you. It sets the rules of engagement — how you communicate, what you will and won't do, and how Claude should behave in every interaction.

  • Your tone and writing style — how you sound, what to avoid
  • Your professional limits — what you don't do or discount
  • How you like to work — process and preferences
  • What Claude should never say or assume about your clients
  • Your communication rules and response style
🧠
my-business-brain.md
Your Business

Where your business actually lives. Everything Claude needs to represent you accurately — your offers, prices, clients, and current focus. This is the file that makes the difference between generic and genuinely useful.

  • Your services and packages with exact pricing
  • Active clients and what you're doing for them right now
  • Your ideal client profile and the problems you solve
  • Current offers, promotions, or availability windows
  • Business context — where you are and what you're focused on
✨
my-prompts.md
Your Best Prompts

Your most-used prompts — written once, saved forever. Stop recreating from memory. This file stores your best requests so you paste and run instead of starting from scratch every week.

  • Your client proposal prompt — in your exact offer format
  • Your reply-to-inquiry prompt — your voice, your call to action
  • Your content writing prompts — the ones that sound like you
  • Prompts for your most common tasks, pre-loaded and ready
  • Any custom requests you've refined and want to keep forever
