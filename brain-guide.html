The Solution

Three files. Everything Claude needs to know.

The Business Brain Workshop walks you through building three files that live in your project folder. Once they exist, Claude reads them automatically at the start of every session — no re-explaining, no setup, no guessing.

📋
CLAUDE.md
Your Rules & Voice

This is Claude's instruction manual for working with you. It sets the rules of engagement — how you communicate, what you will and won't do, and how Claude should behave in every interaction.

  • Your tone and writing style — how you sound, what to avoid
  • Your professional boundaries — what you don't do, what you don't discount
  • How you like to work — your process, your preferences
  • What Claude should never say or assume
  • Your communication rules with clients

my-business-brain.md
Your Business

This is where your business actually lives. Everything Claude needs to represent you accurately — your offers, your prices, your clients, your current focus. This is the file that makes the difference between generic and genuinely useful.

  • Active offers, promotions, or availability windows
  • Business context — where you are right now, what you're focused on

my-prompts.md
Your Best Prompts

The prompts you use most — written once, saved forever. No more recreating from memory. This file stores your go-to requests so you can paste and run instead of writing from scratch every time.

How the Workshop Works

Four steps. Your brain is built.

Step One

Answer Questions

Claude walks you through a structured set of questions about your business — your services, prices, clients, voice, rules, and preferences. You answer in plain language. No template to fill out, no guessing what to include.

Step Two

Build the Files

Based on your answers, Claude drafts all three files — CLAUDE.md, my-business-brain.md, and my-prompts.md. It organizes everything into the right structure so the files are immediately usable.
